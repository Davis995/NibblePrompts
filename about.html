About Nibble Prompts

Nibble Prompts is a static, no-backend prompt reference library. Its core value proposition is providing structured layout descriptions (not style descriptions) that work as AI prompts. Every prompt ends with the same style-matching sentence, making them composable with any screenshot.

How it works (3-Step Guide)

  1. Pick a layout structure: Browse or search through our collection of UI layouts.
  2. Copy the prompt: Click the "Copy prompt" button to grab the structural description.
  3. Paste into your AI tool: Paste the prompt into Claude, ChatGPT, or Cursor, and attach a screenshot of your desired visual aesthetic. The AI will generate a component that matches both the layout structure and your visual style.
